
1 large onion chopped
2 tins tomatoes
1 tbsp paprika
0.5 tsp chilli powder (optional)
2 teaspoons sugar
1 tin butterbeans (drained)
1 medium butternut, cubed and roasted
250ml cream/coconut milk/yogurt
Salt and pepper to taste

Saute onions until translucent. Add the spices and stir for a few moments then add 
the tomatoes and the sugar and simmer for 10 mins. Add the beans and simmer for 5 minutes.
Add the roasted butternut and the cream/coconut milk/yogurt and bring back to the boil. 
Adjust seasoning with salt and pepper then remove from heat and serve on rice or couscous.
