Coriander and lime marinade

1 cup plain yoghurt
2 tbsps lime juice (use more if desired)
1 tbsp ground coriander
2 tsp minced fresh ginger
1 tsp ground cumin
1 tsp salt
4 cloves garlic crushed

Mix ingredients together and toss meat pieces in the marinade until
they are coated. Cover and put in the fridge for up to 24 hours.
